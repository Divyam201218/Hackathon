Campaigns




Campaign NameRole
Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission)Launched in 2014, this campaign aims to improve hygiene, eliminate open defecation, and provide universal sanitation.
Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM)Launched in 2019, this campaign aims to provide piped water to every rural household by 2024
Nirmal Bharat AbhiyanLaunched in 2012, this campaign was renamed rural sanitation.
National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP)This campaign was reformed and incorporated under JJM in 2019 to provide Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) to every rural household by 2024. .
